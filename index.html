Tool

A rock band from Los Angeles

The beggining of the band

Band members moved to Los Angeles in the 80's. At first, Maynard Keenan and Adam Jones met each other through an mutual friend. They all worked in different jobs before that.

After a while, Danny Carey offered to play in the band, because he felt bad that no other drummer would join the band.

They're first album, called Undertow was marked with heavy metal style, what was going to change forever after through next albums.

"If I'd been ranting and raving for years, you wouldn't be listening to me right now"

Maynard James Keenan
